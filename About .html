About Us

Knoetzie Kamma offers a peaceful getaway.

There are five rooms and three cottages available for lodging. Each of the two cottages has two bedrooms, a kitchen equipped with a refrigerator, microwave, stove, and oven, a lounge space, and a bathroom with a shower. The third cottage features a kitchen with a stove, microwave, and refrigerator, as well as a bedroom and a shower-only bathroom.


The Knoetzie Kamma rooms sleep two adults and one child up to twelve years old and have a double bed and a single bed. There is a shower in the en suite bathroom.


Additional amenities include WiFi, a TV with DStv, and a kitchenette with basic dining and heating appliances such a microwave and small refrigerator. There is a shared kitchen and outdoor braai area available to all visitors. Every apartment features a separate balcony or veranda with a picnic bench on the balcony.


If agreed in advance, small, well-trained pets with their own bedding are welcome. Visitors can drive along the Garden Route and visit one of the numerous nearby beaches, or they can explore the town and everything it has to offer.
