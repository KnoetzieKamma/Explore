Die Melkstal Mansions

One youngster up to the age of twelve and two adults can fit in each room. Each room has a double bed and a single bed, and the bathrooms are equipped with a shower, a toilet, and a sink.

A bar fridge and microwave are among the heat-and-eat amenities found in a basic kitchenette. Every room has access to an outside braai area and a shared kitchen where guests can prepare meals. There is Wi-Fi and a TV with a few satellite channels.

A private verandah with a picnic bench is accessible from each room.

Botha se Plaashuis

The Botha se Plaashuis can accommodate up to four adult guests and two youngsters. The cottage has two bedrooms, a day bed in the open area, and one bathroom with a shower, sink, and toilet.

A fully equipped kitchen with a gas stove, an interior fireplace, and an open-concept living space lead to a wooden deck with a river view.
